Local Events

🌿 Market

Rose Ave Farmers Market

Every Saturday morning, Rose Ave turns into a vibrant hub of organic produce, fresh flowers, and local food trucks. Bring your tote bag.
📅 Sat 8:00–1:00 📍 Rose Ave & Main St
🧘 Wellness

Salty Somatics Beach Yoga

Start your day with a grounding flow on Windward Beach. All levels welcome. Mats available for rent.
📅 Sun 9:00 AM 📍 Windward Beach
🎨 Art Walk

Abbot Kinney First Fridays

Gallery openings, pop‑up shops, and live music along the mile. The street comes alive with creativity every first Friday.
📅 First Fri 6:00–10:00 📍 Abbot Kinney Blvd
🏄 Surf

Venice Surf Co. Group Lesson

Beginner and intermediate group surf lessons at the Breakwater. All gear provided. Small groups for personalized coaching.
📅 Daily 7:00 AM 📍 Breakwater Beach
🎵 Live Music

Townhouse Sunday Sessions

Intimate live performances every Sunday evening at the historic Townhouse Venice. Local jazz, folk, and indie artists.
📅 Sun 7:00 PM 📍 Townhouse Venice
🏃 Run Club

Boardwalk Run Club

Meet at Muscle Beach for a 5k loop along the boardwalk. All paces welcome. Coffee and camaraderie after.
📅 Mon/Wed/Fri 7:30 AM 📍 Muscle Beach
📚 Workshop

Timewarp Records Listening Party

Vinyl lovers gather to spin new releases and rare finds. BYO records or just come to listen. Snacks and drinks provided.
📅 Tue 7:00 PM 📍 Timewarp Records

Dogtown Brewing + Taco Truck

Every Friday evening, Dogtown Brewing hosts a rotating taco truck and live DJ. Perfect way to kick off the weekend.
📅 Fri 5:00–10:00 📍 Dogtown Brewing
